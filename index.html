Welcome to Anchor Arms Fitness – Where You’ll Sting Like a Jellyfish and Flex Like a Kraken!

Anchor Arms Fitness is a retro fitness center chain with an underwater twist. We offer a variety of fitness classes, including "Jellyfish Jumps" (high-intensity cardio), "Coral Crunches" (core workouts) and a "Flex Like a Krab" area with posing mirrors and retro lights

The Coral Crunch

This vibrant space, inspired by the ocean's depths, is designed to help you sculpt your core and achieve your fitness goals. Immerse yourself in an underwater wonderland as you tackle a variety of core-strengthening exercises, from crunches and planks to twists and leg raises.

Flex Like a Krab

Step into our Flex Like a Krab Mirror Room and unleash your inner fitness superstar! This vibrant space is designed to help you perfect your poses, build confidence, and celebrate your achievements.

Jellyfish Jumps

Jellyfish Jump Cardio Room is a vibrant and energetic space designed to help you get your heart pumping and your endorphins soaring. This cardio haven is equipped with state-of-the-art equipment, including treadmills, ellipticals, stationary bikes, and more.
